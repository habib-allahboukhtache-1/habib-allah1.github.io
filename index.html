Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about my skills, availability, and how I work.

What’s your main tech stack?

I focus on HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS for front-end. I also use Figma for design, Git for version control, and Linux/Ubuntu as my daily development environment.

Are you available for internships or freelance work?

Yes! I’m open to internships and selective freelance projects. Reach out via the Contact section—we can discuss scope, timelines, and how I can contribute.

How do you approach a new project?

I start by understanding goals and users, then create lightweight wireframes or Figma mockups. From there, I build responsive, accessible front-ends iteratively—shipping small improvements and gathering feedback.

Can you work with an existing design system?

Absolutely. I’m comfortable extending or adopting existing design systems and component libraries. I ensure consistency and accessibility while integrating new features.

Do you do back-end development?

My focus is front-end, but I have basic back-end knowledge (Python, C/C++) and can collaborate with back-end teams. For full-stack projects, I’m happy to work alongside a back-end developer.
